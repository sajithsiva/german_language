die Muttersprache

Meaning

mother tongue

Pronunciation

മുട്ടർ ഷ്പ്രാഹ്

Examples

  • Deutsch ist meine Muttersprache. → German is my native language. (or mother tongue)
  • Was ist deine Muttersprache? → What is your native language?

Notes

Mutter + Sprache

Both parts are faminine on their own.
